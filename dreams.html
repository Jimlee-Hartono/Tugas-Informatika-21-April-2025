My Vision

Inspired by Atri from *Atri: My Dear Moments*, I dream of creating a 1:1 humanoid AI companion that feels truly human.

My goal is to build an AI with emotional intelligence, capable of understanding and responding to human feelings, much like Atri’s warmth and companionship.

Through self-study in AI and robotics, I’m working toward a future where technology fosters genuine connections.
